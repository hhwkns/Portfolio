Get to know me.

I'm a digital marketer and writer living in the Pacific Northwest. So how does an English major become a web developer? Professionally, I help build and tell stories about our products and what makes them [special] [enticing] [empowering] for users. I find site optimization, meticulous research, and content creation rewarding and fun; but, understanding how all the pieces fit together—the UX, styling, navigation, and the messaging—that's my jam.
